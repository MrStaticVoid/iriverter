1.2. Playing Videos

In order to play videos, they must be in a very specific format.

H320, H340

Video codec: XviD 1.0.3

Video size: 220x176

Video framerate: Up to 10 FPS

Maximum video bitrate: Up to 500 Kbps

Audio: MP3, up to 128 Kbps, 44100 Hz

PMP120, PMP140

Video codec: Various MPEG 4 codecs

Video size: 320x240, 512x384, 640x480

Video framerate: Up to 30 FPS

Maximum video bitrate: Up to 1500 Kbps

Audio: MP3, up to 192 Kbps, 44100 Hz

iAudio X5

Video codec: Various MPEG4 codecs

Video size: 160x128

Video framerate: Up to 15 FPS

Maximum video bitrate: Up to 256 Kbps

Audio: MP3, up to 128 Kbps, 44100 Hz

iriverter was designed to convert nearly all videos to these formats to take the difficulty out of video playback. If you already have videos in this format you can simply put them on your device. On the H300 series, videos are played just like any other music file through music mode and the file tree. If your video is not in the necessary format, pass it through iriverter as discussed in Chapter 2 and Chapter 3.
