2.2. OSX

  1. Double-click iriverter-VER_osx.zip, where VER is the version number, in the finder.

  2. Navigate to the resulting directory and drag iriverter.app to /Applications.

  3. Double-click iriverter.app to start iriverter and add it to your dock if you want.
